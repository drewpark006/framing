**The Grumble** at `https://www.thegrumble.com/` is the largest professional picture framers' forum online. Primary source for verbatim user opinions on framing software, equipment, vendors, and shop-floor practices.

**When to use it:**
- Verbatim complaints about competitors (LifeSaver, FrameReady, SpecialtySoft, Virtual Framer, FramingPOS) — search "<product name>" + the forum URL.
- Industry sentiment shifts — major events like the September 2025 FrameReady discontinuation announcement got debated here in real time.
- Vocabulary check — framer terminology (mounting techniques, matting, glass types) is used the way framers actually use it, not the way marketing pages use it.

**Adjacent communities** (smaller, less active):
- **The Framers Forum** at `https://theframersforum.com/` — UK-leaning, smaller signal.
- **PPFA Framers' Corner** at `https://www.ppfacorner.com/` — official PPFA forum, lower volume.
- **r/framing** on Reddit — exists but search indexing is poor; little signal in 2026.

**Caveat:** The Grumble skews to established shop owners running larger / more conventional shops. The 2-person back-of-house-framer pain Phil represents is real but less loudly voiced. Don't take silence about that pain as evidence it doesn't exist.
