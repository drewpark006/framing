**WCAF Expo (West Coast Art & Frame Expo)** is the PPFA-affiliated annual industry trade show. Despite "West Coast" in the name, the 2026 event moved to the East Coast for the first time.

- **Dates:** Friday, February 27 – Sunday, March 1, 2026
- **Venue:** Rosen Shingle Creek, Orlando, FL
- **Official site:** events.american-tradeshow.com/wcaf26
- **Exhibitor directory:** wcaf26.smallworldlabs.com/exhibitors

**Why this matters for Verso:** every framing distributor, magazine, and engaged shop owner is in one place over a weekend. It's the highest-density 3 days for industry conversations in the US. Skipping it costs a year of acquisition velocity.

**Verso's 2026 play (per [[project-verso-gtm-pricing]]):** **walk the floor, do not book a booth.** Booths/sponsorships are premature without 10+ paying customers. Drew talks to 30 shops in one weekend; that beats 6 months of email outreach.

**For 2027:** booth conversation worth having if Verso has revenue + happy customers to demo from.

**Adjacent events to track:**
- Future WCAF dates announced annually post-show
- PPFA chapter events (regional, members-only)
- PFM Conferences (Picture Framing Magazine-hosted)
